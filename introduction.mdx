## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="AniList integration" icon="book-open">
    Fetch anime and manga info from AniList — titles, descriptions, ratings, episodes, and more.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Theme song lookup" icon="music">
    Find opening and ending themes for any anime with direct Spotify links.
  </Card>

  <Card title="AniList account linking" icon="link">
    Register your AniList username so your scores and watch status appear in server lookups.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Japanese kana support" icon="languages">
    Search in Japanese kana or kanji for accurate results by original title.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
